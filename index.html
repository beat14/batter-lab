About us

Where dessert meets the lab bench.

Batter Lab started as a little kitchen experiment — what happens when you dip your favorite snacks in pancake batter and drop them in hot oil? Turns out: magic. Today we serve small-batch fried sweets that are crispy outside, soft inside, and always made fresh when you order.

  • Hand-battered to order
